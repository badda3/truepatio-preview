All Services

Everything your property deserves.

Patio Design & Build

Your patio is the heart of your outdoor space. We design and build custom patios using premium pavers, natural flagstone, travertine, and concrete — each layout tailored to your home, your style, and how you want to live outside.

  • Concrete & paver patios
  • Natural flagstone & travertine
  • Permeable paving options
  • Custom borders & inlays
Walkways & Paths

First impressions start at the curb. Our walkways and garden paths are designed to guide, connect, and impress — using materials that hold up to NJ's four seasons while looking better every year.

  • Front entry walkways
  • Garden & backyard paths
  • Stepping stone installations
  • Edging & borders
Retaining Walls

Function and form in every stone. Our retaining walls are engineered for structural integrity and built to enhance the visual flow of your landscape — whether you need to manage a grade or create a terraced garden feature.

  • Block & natural stone walls
  • Tiered & terraced designs
  • Erosion control solutions
  • Seating wall integration
Outdoor Living Areas

Your backyard should do more than exist. We build complete outdoor rooms — fire pits, seating walls, pergola bases, outdoor kitchen prep areas — designed to extend your living space into the open air.

  • Fire pit circles & features
  • Built-in seating walls
  • Pergola & gazebo bases
  • Outdoor kitchen pads
Landscaping & Planting

Great hardscape needs great softscape to match. We provide grading, mulching, planting, and lawn restoration to ensure your new outdoor space is framed the way it deserves to be.

  • Planting bed design
  • Grading & drainage
  • Lawn seeding & restoration
  • Mulch & ground cover
Full Property Packages

When you want to transform the whole picture — front yard, backyard, and everything in between — we handle it all under one roof. One crew, one timeline, one unified design vision executed start to finish.

  • Full front-to-back design
  • Coordinated scheduling
  • Single point of contact
  • Discounted package pricing

Our Story

Built on craft.
Driven by pride.

True Patio LLC was founded right here in New Jersey by tradespeople who believe in doing things the right way — every time, for every client.

Who We Are

True Patio LLC is a New Jersey–based outdoor construction company specializing in patios, walkways, retaining walls, and complete backyard transformations. We started small — with a commitment to quality work and honest communication — and grew entirely through referrals and word of mouth.

Our team brings decades of combined experience in hardscape and landscape construction. We use only premium materials from trusted suppliers, and we take pride in being on time, transparent, and thorough from the first conversation to the final walkthrough.

Every job is a reference job. We treat your property with the same care we'd bring to our own homes. That standard is what makes us different — and what keeps our clients coming back.
